About Akhand Yogdan Association

Akhand Yogdan Association Sultanpur ek non-profit organization hai jo samaj seva, shiksha, swasthya aur yuva sashaktikaran ke kshetra me kaam karti hai. "Service to Society is the Highest Religion" hamara mool mantra hai.

Our Mission

Samaj ke kamjor vargon ko shiksha, swasthya aur rozgar ke avsar pradan karna.

Our Vision

Ek aisa samaj banana jahan har vyakti ko samman aur saman avsar mile.

Our Green Initiatives

Paryavaran bachao, jeevan bachao. Akhand Yogdan Association prakriti sanrakshan ke liye samarpit hai.

Vriksharopan Abhiyan

Har saal 5000+ paudhe lagakar Sultanpur ko hara-bhara banane ka sankalp. School aur gaon me plantation drives.

Swachhata Abhiyan

Nadi, talab aur public places ki safai. Plastic mukt Sultanpur ke liye jagrukta abhiyan chalate hain.

Jal Sanrakshan

Rainwater harvesting aur jal srot sanrakshan. Paani ki har boond keemti hai - is sandesh ko failate hain.
